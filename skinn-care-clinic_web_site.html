Your Skin Deserves Expert Care

Welcome to Dr. Feyza Seid Specialized Skin Care Clinic, where science meets beauty in Bahir Dar's premier dermatology destination. Located in the Nock Building, we bring you cutting-edge skincare solutions delivered with compassion and expertise.
